PREFACE BY HONORARY EDITOR

Azerbaijan Journal of Chemical News covers various development directions of fundamental chemical science and scientipic achievents in the field of working out of high technologies for manufacturing of important chemical products and new materials. The aim of the publishing of the journal is the information exchange in the medium of researches scientists and industrial workers, providing the requirements bringing together the areas of chemistry and etc. Scientific publications of this journal will help to discover original approaches to the interpretation of scientific experiments, find solution ways of chemical problems and discuss the obtained results. We’ll continue to highlight the relevance of chemistry to everyday life. We make every effort to keep up update with recommendations put forward. We kindly invite all researches in the field of engineering and technological sciences to collaborate with the journal and submit the results of new, interesting researches. The Azerbaijan Journal of Chemical News is published biannual and available online.
